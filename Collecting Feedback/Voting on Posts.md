# Voting on posts

> [!note] This article is written for end users visiting a FlowPlanner-powered board.

Voting is the fastest way to tell a product team you want something. You don't need to submit a new post -- if someone else has already asked for the same thing, ==your vote on their post is equally powerful==.

> [!quote] One vote, real impact
> Product teams sort by vote count to decide what to build next. Your vote is a direct input to their prioritization.

## How to vote

1. Find a post you care about -- browse the list or use search
2. Click the **vote button** (the upward arrow or count on the left side of the post)
3. That's it -- your vote is registered immediately

---

## Voting rules

- **One vote per post** -- you can't vote twice on the same post
- **Votes are not transferable** -- once you vote on a post, you can't move that vote to a different one (you can remove it)
- **Removing a vote** -- click the vote button again to remove your vote

> [!note] Sign-in may be required
> Some boards require you to be signed in to vote. If prompted, create a free account or sign in with your existing one.

---

## Why voting matters more than commenting

Comments explain the request in more detail. Votes tell the team ==how many people want the same thing==. Both are valuable, but if you only have 10 seconds, vote.

> [!tip] Share posts to get more votes
> If a post represents something you really want, share the link with teammates or in community channels. More votes from real users accelerates when it gets built.
