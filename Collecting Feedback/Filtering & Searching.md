# Filtering & searching

With hundreds of posts on an active board, filtering and search help you find exactly what you're looking for -- whether you're a user checking if your idea already exists, or an admin triaging a specific type of request.

> [!quote] Search before you submit
> The most common reason for duplicate posts is users not finding an existing one. A quick ==search before submitting== keeps the board clean and votes consolidated.

## Searching posts

The **search bar** at the top of the feedback board does a full-text search across post titles and descriptions.

- Search is **live** -- results update as you type
- It searches across all categories and statuses
- Click any result to open the full post

---

## Filtering by category

The category filter bar below the search lets you narrow posts to a single category:

1. Click a category name in the filter bar (e.g., "Feature Request")
2. The list updates to show only posts in that category
3. Click the category again (or **All**) to clear the filter

---

## Filtering by status

You can filter the feedback list by status:

- **Open** -- unreviewed or under consideration
- **Planned** -- committed to
- **In Progress** -- being built now
- **Released** -- shipped
- **Closed** -- not building

> [!tip] Use the roadmap tab for Planned/In Progress/Released
> If you want a visual snapshot of what's coming, the **Roadmap** tab already organizes posts into those three statuses automatically.

---

## Sorting options

Posts can be sorted by:

- **Top** (default) -- highest vote count first
- **New** -- most recently submitted first
- **Trending** -- recently active posts rising faster
