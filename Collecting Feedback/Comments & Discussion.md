# Comments & discussion

> [!note] This article is written for end users visiting a FlowPlanner-powered board.

Comments are where the conversation about a feedback post happens. Use them to add context to an existing post, ask for clarification, or share how a problem affects you specifically.

> [!quote] Comments add the "why" behind the vote
> Votes tell the team *how many* people want something. Comments tell them ==why and how it matters==.

## Leaving a comment

1. Open a post by clicking on its title
2. Scroll to the **Comments** section at the bottom
3. Type your comment in the text field
4. Click **Post comment**

Your comment appears immediately and is visible to everyone on the board.

---

## What to write in a comment

> [!tip] Comments that help the team
> - **Add your use case** -- "I run a design agency and this would save me 2 hours a week"
> - **Explain the problem** -- "The current workaround is X but it breaks when Y"
> - **Ask clarifying questions** -- "Would this work for the mobile version too?"
> - **Share a workaround** -- if you've found a way to solve the problem in the meantime

---

## Team responses

Product teams use comments to:

- Post status updates ("We're planning this for Q3")
- Ask for more detail ("Can you say more about your use case?")
- Explain decisions when closing a post

When the team responds to a post you've voted on or commented on, you may receive an email notification.

---

## Editing and deleting comments

- **Your own comments** can be edited or deleted from the post view
- **Team members** can delete any comment on the board
