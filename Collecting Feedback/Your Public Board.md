# Your public board

> [!note] This article is written for end users -- the people who visit and use a FlowPlanner-powered feedback board.

Your public board is a place to share ideas, vote on features you want, and follow the product's roadmap. Everything here is open -- you can see what others have asked for and add your voice to the ideas that matter most to you.

> [!quote] Your voice shapes the product
> When you submit feedback or vote on a post, the team ==sees it immediately==. The most-voted ideas are the ones that get built first.

## What you'll find on a board

**The Feedback tab**
All submitted posts, sorted by votes. Filter by category to browse specific types of requests. Use the search bar to find posts before submitting your own.

**The Roadmap tab**
Posts the team has committed to, organized by stage:
- **Planned** -- coming soon
- **In Progress** -- being built right now
- **Released** -- already shipped

---

## Signing in

Some boards require you to sign in before submitting or voting. If prompted:

1. Click **Sign in** or **Create account**
2. Enter your email and choose a password (or use the magic link option for email-based sign-in)
3. Verify your email if this is your first time

> [!tip] One account works across all FlowPlanner boards
> If you use multiple products that run on FlowPlanner, your account follows you. No need to create a new one for each board.

---

## Notifications

If you vote on a post or submit your own, you may receive an email when:

- The status changes (e.g., moves to Planned or Released)
- The team posts an update or comment

You can manage notification preferences in your account settings.
