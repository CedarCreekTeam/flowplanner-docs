# FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about FlowPlanner.

> [!quote] Still have questions?
> If something isn't covered here, [[Welcome to FlowPlanner Help|browse the full docs]] or reach out to support.

---

## Getting started

**Can I use FlowPlanner for free?**
Yes. FlowPlanner has a free tier that lets you run a public feedback board with no credit card required. Paid plans unlock additional features and higher limits.

**Can I change my subdomain after setup?**
Not directly -- subdomain changes require contacting support. Choose carefully when you sign up.

**Can I have multiple boards?**
Each organization has one board. If you need separate boards for different products, create separate organizations.

---

## Feedback & voting

**Can users submit feedback anonymously?**
This depends on your [[Board Settings|sign-in settings]]. If you allow submissions without an account, users can post without identifying themselves. If you require sign-in to submit, all posts are tied to an account.

**Can the same user vote more than once on a post?**
No. Each account gets one vote per post. Anonymous votes are tied to the browser session.

**Can I delete a post from a user?**
Yes. Admins can delete any post from the admin panel. The user who submitted it won't be notified.

**Can I merge duplicate posts?**
Not as an automatic feature -- but you can manually close duplicate posts and add a comment linking to the canonical post.

## Admin & team

**How many team members can I invite?**
This depends on your plan. Check [[Subscription & Billing]] for current limits.

**What's the difference between admin roles?**
See [[Roles & Permissions]] for a full breakdown of owner, admin, and member permissions.

**Can users see who voted on a post?**
Admins can see voter counts. The specific identities of voters are not publicly visible to other users.

## The roadmap

**Does my roadmap update automatically?**
Yes. When you change the status of a post to Planned, In Progress, or Released, it moves to the corresponding column on your public roadmap automatically.

**Can I hide the roadmap from the public?**
The roadmap is visible to anyone who can view your board. If your board is public, your roadmap is public. You can control what appears there by how you manage statuses.

---

## Billing & account

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to **Account → Subscription & Billing** and click **Cancel plan**. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

**What happens to my data if I cancel?**
Your board, posts, and votes remain intact. You'll be moved to the free tier limits. Data is not deleted on cancellation.

**Can I export my feedback data?**
Export is available on paid plans via the API. See [[API Reference/Introduction|API docs]] for details.
