# Creating your account

Getting started with FlowPlanner takes less than two minutes. You'll create an account, set up your organization, and choose your board's subdomain.

> [!quote] Your board, your way
> Your FlowPlanner board lives at ==yourname.flowplanner.io== -- pick a subdomain that matches your product.

## Sign up

1. Go to **[flowplanner.io](https://flowplanner.io)** and click **Start for free**
2. Enter your **email address** and choose a **password**
3. Click **Create account**
4. Check your inbox for a **verification email** and click the link inside

> [!tip] Magic link option
> Prefer not to use a password? Click **Email link** on the sign-in page and we'll send you a one-click login link instead.

---

## Set up your organization

After verifying your email, you'll be prompted to set up your organization:

1. Enter your **name**
2. Enter your **board name** -- this is what appears as the title on your public board (e.g., "TickUp Feedback")
3. Choose a **subdomain** -- your board will be available at `yoursubdomain.flowplanner.io`

> [!note] Choosing your subdomain
> Use your product name or company name, all lowercase. Once set, the subdomain ==cannot be changed== without contacting support. Choose carefully.

---

## After setup

Once your organization is created, you'll land on your **dashboard**. From here you can:

- Visit your public board to see what it looks like
- Open the **admin panel** to configure categories, settings, and team members
- Share your board URL with users to start collecting feedback

> [!success] Your board is live immediately
> No approval process, no waiting period. Your board is public and ready for feedback the moment you finish setup.
