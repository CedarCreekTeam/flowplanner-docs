# Board settings

Board settings let you control how your board looks, who can access it, and what visitors can do. Go to **Admin → Settings** to configure these options.

> [!quote] A well-configured board earns more trust
> Clear naming, a good description, and the right sign-in settings ==lower the barrier== for users to contribute.

## Board identity

**Board name**
The title shown in the header of your public board. Use your product name followed by "Feedback" -- e.g., "TickUp Feedback" or "Acme Feedback Board."

**Description**
A short sentence shown below your board name on the public page. Tell visitors what to do and why it matters.

> [!tip] Good descriptions
> - "Share what you'd like to see in TickUp. Vote on ideas from other users. Follow our roadmap."
> - "Tell us what to build next. Your votes help us prioritize."

---

## Sign-in requirements

Control what visitors must do before they can participate:

| Setting | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Anyone can submit** | No account required to submit a post |
| **Sign in to submit** | Account required to submit; browsing is open |
| **Sign in to vote** | Account required to vote; browsing is open |
| **Sign in for everything** | Account required for all interactions |

> [!note] Sign-in reduces spam but also reduces volume
> Requiring sign-in produces fewer, higher-quality submissions. Allowing anonymous submissions gets you more raw signal but requires more moderation. Start open; restrict if you see spam.

---

## Board visibility

**Public** (default)
Anyone with the URL can view your board and roadmap. No sign-in required to browse.

**Private**
Only invited team members can view the board. Use this for internal boards or during pre-launch phases.

---

## Subdomain

Your board URL (`yoursubdomain.flowplanner.io`) is set at account creation. It cannot be changed from the settings page. Contact support if you need a subdomain change.

---

## Deleting your organization

At the bottom of the Settings page, the **Delete organization** option permanently removes your board, all feedback, all votes, and all member associations.

> [!note] Deletion is permanent
> There is no recovery after deleting an organization. Export your data via the API first if you need a record of your feedback history.
