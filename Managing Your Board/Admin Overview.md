# Admin overview

The admin panel is your command center for managing feedback. Every post, every category, every team member, and every setting is controlled from here.

> [!quote] Triage is a habit, not a task
> The teams who get the most value from FlowPlanner ==check the admin panel regularly== -- not just when they're about to ship something.

## Accessing the admin panel

Go to `yoursubdomain.flowplanner.io/admin` or click **Admin** in the board header when signed in as an admin or owner.

---

## Admin panel sections

### Overview
A summary dashboard showing:
- Total feedback posts
- Posts by status
- Top categories
- Quick action links

### Feedback
The full list of all submitted posts, with filtering, sorting, and bulk actions. This is where most of your day-to-day triage happens.

## Your daily workflow

> [!tip] A 5-minute admin routine
> 1. Open **Feedback** and filter by **Open** -- review anything new
> 2. Set a status and category on each new post
> 3. Leave a comment if the post needs clarification or a quick acknowledgment
> 4. Move anything you're actively building to **In Progress**

> [!success] The triage mindset
> Your goal isn't to respond to every post in depth -- it's to ==make sure nothing sits uncategorized for long==. Users who submit feedback and see a status set within a few days feel heard, even if the answer isn't "yes."
