# Inviting team members

Bring your teammates into FlowPlanner so everyone on your team can review feedback, update statuses, and respond to users -- without sharing a single login.

> [!quote] Feedback management shouldn't fall on one person
> When your whole team can triage and respond, ==nothing sits unreviewed==. Shared access means shared accountability.

## Inviting a team member

1. Go to **Admin → Team**
2. Click **Invite member**
3. Enter the team member's **email address**
4. Select their **role** -- Admin or Member
5. Click **Send invite**

They'll receive an email invitation with a link to join your organization. If they don't have a FlowPlanner account yet, they'll be prompted to create one.

> [!note] Invitations expire after 7 days
> If a team member doesn't accept within a week, you'll need to send a new invitation from the Team page.

---

## Choosing a role

| Role | Can do |
|------|--------|
| **Admin** | Full access: triage, settings, invite others |
| **Member** | Triage and respond -- no settings access |

Choose Admin for people who own the product feedback process. Choose Member for teammates (support, engineering) who need read/write access to posts but shouldn't be changing board configuration.

## Managing existing members

From **Admin → Team** you can:

- **Change a member's role** -- click their name and select a new role
- **Remove a member** -- click **Remove** next to their name

> [!note] Removing a member is immediate
> When you remove someone, their access is revoked instantly. Any posts or comments they made remain on the board.

---

## The owner role

The organization owner is the account that created the organization. The owner:

- Has all Admin permissions
- Is the only one who can manage billing
- Cannot be removed by other admins
- Can transfer ownership (contact support)
