# Account settings

Manage your personal profile, display name, avatar, and theme preferences from your account settings page.

> [!quote] Your account, your preferences
> Settings you change here apply to your account ==across all FlowPlanner boards== you participate in.

## Accessing account settings

Click your avatar or name in the top-right corner of any FlowPlanner page and select **Profile** or **Settings**.

---

## Profile settings

**Display name**
The name shown when you comment on posts or appear in admin views. Use your real name or a recognizable handle.

**Email address**
The email associated with your account. Used for sign-in and notifications. Contact support to change your email address.

**Avatar**
Upload a profile photo that appears next to your comments and in the admin team list. Square images work best; FlowPlanner will crop to a circle.

---

## Theme preference

FlowPlanner supports **dark mode** and **light mode**. Toggle your preference using the theme button in any board header.

Your theme preference is ==saved to your account== and applies on every device and across all FlowPlanner boards you visit while signed in.

---

## Notification preferences

Control when FlowPlanner sends you email notifications:

- **Status changes** on posts you've voted on or submitted
- **Comments** on your posts or threads you've engaged with
- **Team mentions** (when an admin tags you in a comment)

---

## Password

Change your password from **Account Settings → Security**. You'll need to enter your current password to set a new one.

If you signed up with a magic link (no password), you can set a password from this page.
