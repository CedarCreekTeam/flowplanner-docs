# Password reset

If you can't sign in to FlowPlanner, you can reset your password by email.

> [!quote] Locked out? You're back in under 2 minutes
> FlowPlanner sends a secure reset link to your email address. ==No security questions, no delays.==

## Reset your password

1. Go to [flowplanner.io/login](https://flowplanner.io/login)
2. Click **Forgot password?**
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Click **Send reset link**
5. Check your inbox for an email from FlowPlanner
6. Click the **Reset password** link in the email
7. Enter and confirm your new password
8. Click **Save** -- you're signed in automatically

> [!tip] Check your spam folder
> Reset emails occasionally land in spam. If you don't see it within 2 minutes, check your spam or junk folder.

---

## Magic link users

If you originally signed up with a magic link (no password), you don't have a password to reset. Instead:

1. Go to [flowplanner.io/login](https://flowplanner.io/login)
2. Click **Email link**
3. Enter your email
4. Click the link in the email to sign in

If you want to set a password on your account, do so from **Account Settings → Security** once you're signed in.

---

## If the reset email never arrives

- Make sure you're using the exact email you signed up with
- Check spam, junk, and any email filters
- The reset link expires after **1 hour** -- request a new one if yours expired
- If you're still stuck, contact support
