# Subscription & billing

FlowPlanner offers a free tier and paid plans. The organization owner manages billing from **Account → Subscription**.

> [!quote] Start free, grow when you're ready
> FlowPlanner's free plan is genuinely useful -- not a limited trial. ==Upgrade when you need more==, not before.

## Plans

| Plan | Who it's for |
|------|-------------|
| **Free** | One board, core features, limited posts and team members |
| **Pro** | Unlimited posts, more team members, advanced settings |
| **Business** | Higher limits, API access, webhooks, priority support |

For current pricing, visit [flowplanner.io](https://flowplanner.io).

---

## Upgrading your plan

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Subscription** (as the organization owner)
2. Click **Upgrade plan**
3. Select your plan
4. Enter your payment details and confirm

Your board upgrades immediately. You're billed monthly or annually depending on the option you choose. Annual plans offer a discount.

---

## Downgrading your plan

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Subscription**
2. Click **Change plan**
3. Select the lower plan

Downgrading takes effect at the end of your current billing period. Your board and data are not affected -- you'll simply lose access to features above your new plan's limits.

---

## Cancelling

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel plan**
3. Confirm the cancellation

Access continues until the end of your billing period. Your data is not deleted -- you'll be moved to free tier limits.

---

## Invoices

Past invoices are available in **Admin → Settings → Subscription → Billing history**. Each invoice is a downloadable PDF.

---

## Payment methods

FlowPlanner accepts major credit and debit cards via Stripe. We do not store card numbers -- all payment processing is handled by Stripe.

To update your payment method, go to **Admin → Settings → Subscription → Payment method**.
