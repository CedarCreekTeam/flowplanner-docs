# Welcome to FlowPlanner Help

FlowPlanner gives your product a **public feedback board and roadmap** -- so your users can tell you what they need, vote on what matters most, and watch ideas become reality.

> [!quote] Build what your users actually want
> Stop guessing. FlowPlanner gives your users a ==direct line to your roadmap== -- and gives you the signal you need to build the right things.
