# Sharing your roadmap

Your roadmap has a permanent public URL you can share anywhere -- in your marketing site, release announcements, newsletters, or social posts.

> [!quote] A shared roadmap is a recruiting tool
> Users who follow your roadmap become invested in your product's success. They ==root for you to ship== the things they voted on.

## Your roadmap URL

Your roadmap is always available at:

```
https://yoursubdomain.flowplanner.io/roadmap
```

This URL links directly to the Roadmap tab on your board.

---

## Where to share your roadmap

> [!success] High-impact places to link your roadmap
> - **Your app's footer or navigation** -- "See what we're building →"
> - **Release announcement emails** -- link to Released items alongside your changelog
> - **Your marketing site's pricing page** -- shows prospects that you're actively developing
> - **Social posts when you ship** -- "We shipped [feature] -- see what's next on our roadmap"
> - **Your README or docs** -- for developer-facing products

---

## Using the roadmap in announcements

When you ship something, update the status to Released and share the roadmap link. Users who voted on that post will see their idea in the Released column alongside everything else you're planning.

> [!tip] Combine your roadmap with a short update
> "We just shipped [X]. It started as a feedback request with 87 votes. See what's coming next: [roadmap link]"
>
> This pattern ==closes the feedback loop publicly== and invites more votes on what comes next.

---

## Embedding your roadmap

FlowPlanner doesn't currently support iframe embedding. Link directly to your roadmap URL. If embed support is something you need, [[Submitting Feedback|submit it as feedback →]] on our own board.
